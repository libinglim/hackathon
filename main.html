Wellness at School

As students, it's important to take care of our physical and mental health so we can perform our best in school. Here are some tips for staying healthy:

  • Get enough sleep (at least 8 hours a night)
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Take breaks and move around during long periods of sitting
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques such as deep breathing or meditation
  • Stay connected with friends and family for emotional support
  • Reach out to a trusted adult or counselor if you're struggling with mental health issues

Get Enough Sleep

Getting at least 8 hours of sleep each night is essential for good physical and mental health. It helps improve memory and concentration, reduces stress, and boosts immune function.
